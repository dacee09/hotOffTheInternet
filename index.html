Software Developer

My name is Candace Campos and I got into software development because of games and my friends. When I was young, I was only ever interested in using technology and that was it. I wasn't curious about it or found it interesting, it was just a tool for me. However in highschool, I bought my first laptop and I started playing League of Legends. I was in a magnet school for math, science, and technology, so a lot of my friends were interested in computers and becoming software developers in the future. When I had to decide what to do for college, I just thought, "I like video games and I like computers so I guess I'll just do CS." However, that dismissive comment actually became one of my biggest interests and what connected me further with my friends. Now I'm very curious and I have so much I want to learn to better my abilities and create things.
